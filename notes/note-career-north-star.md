# Career north star

An example **career-wide** note: the kind of prose that belongs at the top layer of the hub unit because
it spans the whole domain rather than any single course or project. It binds directly to [[hub-career]]
(a member via `related`), so it renders with the career hub at `standard` — unlike a course note, which
lives one layer down under [[hub-courses]].

Replace this with your own north-star statement; it exists to demonstrate that a parent hub can carry a
little of its own knowledge *and* navigate to sub-hubs at the same time
